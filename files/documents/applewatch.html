    Monumental display.

    Series 10 is a major milestone for AppleWatch. It features our biggest and most advanced display yet,3 showing more information onscreen than ever. With Apples first wide-angle OLED Display, the screen is brighter when viewed from an angle, making it easier to read with a quickglance.

    Sleek form.

    Series10 is our thinnest watch ever.3 To make this possible, we re-engineered nearly every aspect of it, including the SiP, Digital Crown, speaker, front crystal, and antenna. It all comes together to create a watch thats packed full ofinnovation.

    Series 7, 8, 9

    Health insights for days. And nights.

    From sleep tracking to heart health notifications and much more, the advanced sensors in Series10 help you better understand yourhealth.

    Sleep apnea is a condition where breathing stops repeatedly and causes disrupted sleep. If untreated, it can lead to hypertension, type 2 diabetes, and cardiac issues. You can be notified if signs of sleep apnea are detected and receive a report that helps you navigate conversations with your healthcare provider.5

    80%
    of sleep apnea cases are undiagnosed5

    • Sleep on it. Get help setting up a bedtime routine and tracking your sleep. See the time you spent in REM, Core, and Deep sleep, as well as when you might have woken up. You can even get 8 hours of sleep tracking with just an 8‑minutecharge.6

    • Listen to your heart. With the ECG app, AppleWatch Series10 is capable of generating an ECG similar to a singlelead electrocardiogram.7 And with the Heart Rate app, you can get notifications for high and low heart rate as well as an irregularrhythm.8

    • Vital metrics. The Vitals app helps you identify changes in your daily health status.9 Quickly see your key overnight health metrics right on your wrist including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, and sleep duration.

    • Understand your cycle. AppleWatch has an innovative sensor that tracks your temperature while you sleep.10 The Cycle Tracking app uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you likely ovulated, which can be helpful for familyplanning.11

    Fit for anything.

    Series10 is the perfect fitness partner. It motivates you to stay active, tracks all your workouts, and gives you the metrics youneed.

    The water temperature sensor gives you more information for your swim workouts. And a depth sensor makes Series10 great for swimming and snorkeling.12 So go on, make asplash.

    • Exercise your options. From running and strength training to Pilates and dance, AppleWatch delivers a healthy number of ways to work out and all the metrics you couldwant.

    • Run rings around your day. The Activity app tracks your fitness goals and encourages you to close your Move, Exercise, and Stand rings every day. You can easily pause your Activity rings or adjust your goals for each day of theweek.

    • Work it out with training load. See how the intensity and duration of your workouts may impact your body over time. So you can make an educated decision on when to push or when torecover.

    • Sea the Tides app. Check conditions for over 115,000 beaches and 5,000 surf spots around the world, including high and low tides, rising and falling tides, and sunrise andsunset.

    • Your snorkeling buddy. The Oceanic+ app on Series10 has fun snorkeling features.13 While underwater, youll be able to track your time, maximum depth, direction, and othermetrics.

    • AppleWatch plus Fitness+. Using AppleWatch with Fitness+ lets you see your realtime metrics onscreen. And you get the freedom of audioguided walks, runs, and meditations when you dont have your iPhone withyou.

    Intelligent ways to stay connected.

    Stay connected to the people and things that you care about most. Cellular lets you keep in touch, even when you dont have your iPhone nearby.14 And intelligent features help you stay informed and on top ofeverything.

    • Smarts that stack up. The SmartStack automatically shows relevant information throughout the day based on time, location, and more. Like if rain is coming. LiveActivities can keep you updated on things like sports scores or your upcomingflight.

    • Words travel fast. The Translate app automatically adds a widget to your Smart Stack when youre traveling somewhere a different language is spoken. You can even download languages to your watch so you can use Translate without your iPhone, Wi‑Fi, or a cellular connection.15

    • Go without your phone. Take a call from the trail. Return a text on your run. Cellular lets you leave your phone behind without missing abeat.14

    • Always have money on hand. Pay from your wrist with ApplePay,16or send money to friends with AppleCash.17You can also use the Wallet app as your ID, home key, car key, boarding pass, and transitcard.18

    • No more hide and seek. PrecisionFinding directs you to your iPhone by showing you its distance and direction.19 When you get close, haptic feedback, an audible chirp, and a visual indicator help you zero in onit.

    • Hear here. Easily stream music, podcasts, and audiobooks with AirPods. If you dont have headphones nearby and want to listen to something quickly, you can now play audio through the AppleWatchspeaker.

    • More apps in a tap. Get access to tens of thousands of apps with the App Store. Its a great way to customize your watch for whatever you love todo.

    Keep help
    close at hand.

    Life is full of the unexpected. Thats why AppleWatch is there to call for help when you need it most. It can even let someone know when youve safely made it to adestination.

    Need help? AppleWatch has your back. Just press and hold the side button to call emergency services and share yourlocation.20

    AppleWatch can automatically detect if youve taken a hard fall or been in a severe car crash. If you need help, it will call emergency services, share your location, and notify your emergency contacts.20

    Check In notifies someone when you arrive at a destination. If youre going for a run after dark, you can start a Check In for the duration of your workout to automatically notify a friend when youredone.21

    Designed tomake adifference.

    What matters to you matters to Apple, too. From using more recycled materials that minimize environmental impact. To privacy protections that give you more control over your data. To creating builtin features that make AppleWatch accessible toall.

    Series10 cases are made with 100% recycled aluminum or 95% recycled titanium. This year, you can configure any AppleWatch in any material or finish to be carbonneutral.22

    Monumental display.

    Series 10 is a major milestone for Apple Watch. It features our biggest and most advanced display yet,3 showing more information onscreen than ever. With Apple’s first wide-angle OLED Display, the screen is brighter when viewed from an angle, making it easier to read with a quick glance.

    Sleek form.

    Series 10 is our thinnest watch ever.3 To make this possible, we re-engineered nearly every aspect of it, including the SiP, Digital Crown, speaker, front crystal, and antenna. It all comes together to create a watch that’s packed full of innovation.

    Series 7, 8, 9

    Series 10

    Series 10 has the largest screen area of any Apple Watch. It has up to 75% more screen area than Series 3, up to 30% more screen area than Series 4, 5, 6, and SE, and up to 9% more screen area than Series 7, 8, and 9. So you can see and do even more.

    Health insights for days. And nights.

    From sleep tracking to heart health notifications and much more, the advanced sensors in Series 10 help you better understand your health.

    Sleep apnea is a condition where breathing stops repeatedly and causes disrupted sleep. If untreated, it can lead to hypertension, type 2 diabetes, and cardiac issues. You can be notified if signs of sleep apnea are detected and receive a report that helps you navigate conversations with your healthcare provider.5

    80%
    of sleep apnea cases are undiagnosed5

    • Sleep on it. Get help setting up a bedtime routine and tracking your sleep. See the time you spent in REM, Core, and Deep sleep, as well as when you might have woken up. You can even get 8 hours of sleep tracking with just an 8‑minute charge.6

    • Listen to your heart. With the ECG app, Apple Watch Series 10 is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single‑lead electrocardiogram.7 And with the Heart Rate app, you can get notifications for high and low heart rate as well as an irregular rhythm.8

    • Vital metrics. The Vitals app helps you identify changes in your daily health status.9 Quickly see your key overnight health metrics right on your wrist — including heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, and sleep duration.

    • Understand your cycle. Apple Watch has an innovative sensor that tracks your temperature while you sleep.10 The Cycle Tracking app uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you likely ovulated, which can be helpful for family planning.11

    Fit for anything.

    Series 10 is the perfect fitness partner. It motivates you to stay active, tracks all your workouts, and gives you the metrics you need.

    The water temperature sensor gives you more information for your swim workouts. And a depth sensor makes Series 10 great for swimming and snorkeling.12 So go on, make a splash.

    • Exercise your options. From running and strength training to Pilates and dance, Apple Watch delivers a healthy number of ways to work out — and all the metrics you could want.

    • Run rings around your day. The Activity app tracks your fitness goals and encourages you to close your Move, Exercise, and Stand rings every day. You can easily pause your Activity rings or adjust your goals for each day of the week.

    • Work it out with training load. See how the intensity and duration of your workouts may impact your body over time. So you can make an educated decision on when to push or when to recover.

    • Sea the Tides app. Check conditions for over 115,000 beaches and 5,000 surf spots around the world, including high and low tides, rising and falling tides, and sunrise and sunset.

    • Your snorkeling buddy. The Oceanic+ app on Series 10 has fun snorkeling features.13 While underwater, you’ll be able to track your time, maximum depth, direction, and other metrics.

    • Apple Watch plus Fitness+. Using Apple Watch with Fitness+ lets you see your real‑time metrics onscreen. And you get the freedom of audio‑guided walks, runs, and meditations when you don’t have your iPhone with you.

    Intelligent ways to stay connected.

    Stay connected to the people and things that you care about most. Cellular lets you keep in touch, even when you don’t have your iPhone nearby.14 And intelligent features help you stay informed and on top of everything.

    • Smarts that stack up. The Smart Stack automatically shows relevant information throughout the day based on time, location, and more. Like if rain is coming. Live Activities can keep you updated on things like sports scores or your upcoming flight.

    • Words travel fast. The Translate app automatically adds a widget to your Smart Stack when you’re traveling somewhere a different language is spoken. You can even download languages to your watch so you can use Translate without your iPhone, Wi‑Fi, or a cellular connection.15

    • Go without your phone. Take a call from the trail. Return a text on your run. Cellular lets you leave your phone behind without missing a beat.14

    • Always have money on hand. Pay from your wrist with Apple Pay,16 or send money to friends with Apple Cash.17 You can also use the Wallet app as your ID, home key, car key, boarding pass, and transit card.18

    • No more hide and seek. Precision Finding directs you to your iPhone by showing you its distance and direction.19 When you get close, haptic feedback, an audible chirp, and a visual indicator help you zero in on it.

    • Hear here. Easily stream music, podcasts, and audiobooks with AirPods. If you don’t have headphones nearby and want to listen to something quickly, you can now play audio through the Apple Watch speaker.

    • More apps in a tap. Get access to tens of thousands of apps with the App Store. It’s a great way to customize your watch for whatever you love to do.

    Keep help
    close at hand.

    Life is full of the unexpected. That’s why Apple Watch is there to call for help when you need it most. It can even let someone know when you’ve safely made it to a destination.

    Need help? Apple Watch has your back. Just press and hold the side button to call emergency services and share your location.20

    Apple Watch can automatically detect if you’ve taken a hard fall or been in a severe car crash. If you need help, it will call emergency services, share your location, and notify your emergency contacts.20

    Check In notifies someone when you arrive at a destination. If you’re going for a run after dark, you can start a Check In for the duration of your workout to automatically notify a friend when you’re done.21

    Designed to make a difference.

    What matters to you matters to Apple, too. From using more recycled materials that minimize environmental impact. To privacy protections that give you more control over your data. To creating built‑in features that make Apple Watch accessible to all.

    Series 10 cases are made with 100% recycled aluminum or 95% recycled titanium. This year, you can configure any Apple Watch in any material or finish to be carbon neutral.22
